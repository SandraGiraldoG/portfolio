UX/UI designer

I am a UX and UI designer who grew up in the beautiful Colombian mountains. When I was 19 I moved to California where I did my studies in web and mobile design. I started freelancing in 2018 as UI designer and I had the opportunity to work in different projects and in fast paced environments. Throughout these projects I developed an interest to know and understand the user in a deeper level, his/her real motivations and needs so I decided to do a 2.5 months UX/UI Bootcamp which happened to be fully remote.

In my free time, I like hiking, biking, reading, trying new food, and exploring the cute little French towns.
